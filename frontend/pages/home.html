Welcome to Your Imaginary Friend

This web app lets you talk to an AI-powered imaginary friend who listens, responds, and reacts to your voice.

How It Works:

  • Click "Start Speaking" and talk into your microphone.
  • Your speech is recorded, transcribed, and analyzed.
  • An emotion vector is calculated and displayed as an intensity bar.
  • An AI friend generates a thoughtful response that is displayed and spoken back to you.
